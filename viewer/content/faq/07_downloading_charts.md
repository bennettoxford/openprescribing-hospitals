title: Downloading Charts
---

### How do I download a chart?

You can download charts from both the Measures pages and the Analyse tool. To download a chart:

1. Click the menu icon (☰) in the top-right corner of the chart
2. Select **"Download PNG image"** from the dropdown menu

### Can I download the chart data?

You can also download the underlying data for a chart as a CSV file:

1. Click the menu icon (☰) in the top-right corner of the chart
2. Select **"Download CSV"** from the dropdown menu
