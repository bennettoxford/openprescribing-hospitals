Organisation Submissions

About This Chart

This chart visualises the data submission history for organisations within the SCMD. It offers a quick overview of submission patterns, helping to identify gaps and organisational changes over time.

How to Read the Chart

  • Organisations: Listed vertically on the left. Predecessor organisations are shown beneath their successors and are shaded.
  • Time Periods: Displayed horizontally, representing months from left to right.
  • Submission Status: Each square represents a monthly submission:
    • Green: Successful submission
    • Red: Missing submission
  • Group Separators: Horizontal lines divide unrelated organisation groups for improved readability.

For detailed information about a specific submission, hover over any coloured square in the chart.
